Keya (કેયા) Vadgama is a Gujarati-Canadian graphic designer. She received her M.A. in Typeface Design from the University of Reading in 2020. You can follow her work on Instagram. The text of this site is set in Mango, a multilingual typeface designed by Keya.


The site was designed and developed by Neeta Patel, for the course Software for People, part of Yale University’s M.F.A. in Graphic Design.


The transcript presented here is an edited version of a conversation between Keya and Neeta that took place over Zoom on the evening of February 6, 2022.
